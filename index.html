Core Hardware Architecture

Translating features into a physical prototype.

Processing & AI

Main MCU STM32WB series or ESP32-S3 for low-power, high-performance computing.
AI Accelerator Syntiant NDP or ESP32-S3 vector instructions for local anomaly detection.

Security & Sensing

IMU Sensor MPU6050 / LIS3DH to detect tilting, lifting, or shaking.
Door Sensor Magnetic reed / micro-limit switch to log exact access times.
Donation Sensor Load Cell + HX711 to translate weight into cash accumulation estimates.

Connectivity & Access

GSM/LTE Module SIM7600 series 4G LTE for remote cloud syncing & SMS alerts.
Solenoid Lock 12V physical barrier released only via dynamic digital unlock key.
Local Interface OLED Display (SSD1306) + Matrix Keypad or Fingerprint Reader.

Power Management

Battery Pack 3.7V Li-ion or LiFePO4 (e.g., 18650 cells in parallel).
Charging Circuit TP4056 or MPPT solar controller linked to a 5V/12V Solar Panel.

Why DigiPeti?

Bridging the physical and digital worlds.

Over 2.5 million charitable and religious institutions across India handle cash donations without digital oversight — a massive gap for innovation.

While most platforms focus on online donations, DigiPeti modernizes the vast offline donation ecosystem by converting traditional donation boxes into intelligent, AI-powered smart donation boxes. This bridges the gap between physical cash donations and digital management through real-time insights and connected technology.
